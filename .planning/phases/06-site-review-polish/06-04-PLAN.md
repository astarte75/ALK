**Responsive sweep (REVIEW-01):**
For each page, check at 320px viewport width:
- No horizontal scrollbar
- No text overflow or clipping
- Images contained within viewport
- Forms usable (inputs not cut off)
- Navigation accessible

At 2560px:
- Content doesn't stretch to full width
- Comfortable reading experience
- No awkward gaps or alignment issues

Fix any issues found — most likely candidates:
- Tables or grids that don't wrap at mobile
- Images without max-width constraints
- Long words or URLs causing overflow (add `overflow-wrap: break-word` where needed)
- Footer columns at ultra-wide

**Typography consistency (REVIEW-02):**
Compare heading sizes across all pages — they should match:
- h1 (page title): 3rem mobile, 3.75rem desktop
- h2 (section title): consistent across all pages
- h3 (card/subsection): consistent across all pages
- Body text: 1rem, line-height 1.7, Inter
- Accent text: letter-spacing, transform consistent

If GlobalStyle needs typography fixes (base heading sizes, body text), apply there.

**Visual consistency (REVIEW-04):**
- Card styles: PortfolioCard, TeamCard, NewsCard, FundCard all use consistent border, border-radius, surface color
- Section spacing: `padding-top` and `padding-bottom` consistent across all page sections
- Color usage: accentTeal for CTAs/links, accentGold for highlights, correct surface/border colors
- Dark theme contrast: verify no text is hard to read against dark backgrounds

7. Fix any missing translations, stale EN content, or IT-only content that lacks an EN counterpart
